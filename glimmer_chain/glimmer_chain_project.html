Glimmer Chain

What is Glimmer Chain?

Glimmer Chain is a skill-based 2D platformer where you are a robot powered by a hook and chain. Jump and use the chain to complete levels.


The project started in June 2022 and was released in April 9 of 2024. It's developed using a custom engine witch I made from scratch in C++ and the team was made by 3 people: An artists, a music and me.

Why Creating An Engine?

People make me a common question: Why you don't use an existing game engine?
Part is because I really enjoy it and wanted to have a finished and polished product made from scratch in my Portfolio. I wanted to face all the problems and learn from it to be prepared for bigger projects in the future.

There are also practical reasons:

  • You have full control of what the user computer is running and how memory is managed.
  • Tools and the engine adapts to the project, not the way around.
  • For such a simple game you don't need most of the features other game engines have.
  • Most of the core features are reusable for other projects.
